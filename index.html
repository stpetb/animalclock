How This Works

This counter estimates the number of animal lives saved so far this year, based on global human death statistics. The calculation assumes that each human death results in reduced environmental impact and resource consumption, thereby "saving" animal lives that would otherwise be lost to habitat destruction, pollution, and other human-caused factors.
